# Legal Compliance Quick Reference Card

## ‚úÖ Before You Start - Checklist

Use this checklist EVERY TIME before using DDoS Tools:

### Authorization Check
- [ ] I own the target system(s) OR
- [ ] I have written authorization from the owner OR
- [ ] I am in an isolated lab environment with approval

### Documentation
- [ ] I have written authorization (if testing others' systems)
- [ ] I have defined scope and limitations
- [ ] I have emergency contact information
- [ ] I have documented the testing period

### Legal Compliance
- [ ] I have reviewed applicable laws in my jurisdiction
- [ ] I understand the penalties for unauthorized use
- [ ] I am NOT violating any terms of service
- [ ] I have consulted legal counsel (if unsure)

### Safety Checks
- [ ] Target systems are isolated from production (if testing)
- [ ] I have rollback/recovery procedures
- [ ] I have notified relevant stakeholders
- [ ] I will document all activities

**IF YOU CAN'T CHECK ALL BOXES - STOP. DO NOT PROCEED.**

### If You Accidentally Attack Wrong Target
1. **STOP IMMEDIATELY**
2. Document what happened
3. Notify the affected party

5. Consult legal counsel

## ‚öñÔ∏è Penalties (Examples)

### United States (CFAA)
- First offense: Up to 5 years prison + fines
- Repeat offense: Up to 10 years prison + fines
- Causing damage: Up to 20 years prison + fines

### United Kingdom (Computer Misuse Act)
- Unauthorized access: Up to 2 years prison
- With intent to commit further offenses: Up to 5 years
- Unauthorized modification: Up to 10 years

### European Union
- Varies by country: 2-10 years typical
- Additional civil liability
- Criminal record

**These are REAL penalties. People go to prison for this.**

## üí° Remember

**The 5 Second Rule:**
Before executing ANY attack, ask yourself:

1. Do I have written permission? (5 seconds)
2. Is this within authorized scope? (5 seconds)
3. Could this harm someone? (5 seconds)
4. Is this legal in my jurisdiction? (5 seconds)
5. Would I be comfortable explaining this to a judge? (5 seconds)

**If ANY answer is "no" or "unsure" - STOP.**
